ABOUT

Our system is an Automated Library Attendance Monitoring System that utilizes QR code technology to streamline and secure the process of tracking student attendance in the library. This modern solution replaces the traditional manual sign-in methods, enhancing accuracy and efficiency while reducing time and effort for both students and staff.

The system works by assigning each student a unique QR code. Upon entering or exiting the library, students scan their QR code using a designated scanner or device. The system then automatically records the student's information along with the timestamp into a centralized database. This data can be easily accessed and reviewed by administrators for reporting or monitoring purposes.

By automating attendance monitoring, this system reduces human error, minimizes fraudulent entries, and provides real-time data for better management of library usage.
